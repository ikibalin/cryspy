# `setup` item/loop:

     _setup_wavelength     0.84        
     _setup_field          1.00
     _setup_offset_2theta -0.385
     _setup_offset_phi    -0.385
     _setup_ratio_lambda/2 0.           

**Mandatory parameters:** 
`wavelength` in Angstroms.

**Optional parameters:** 
`field` in Tesla,
`offset_2theta` in degrees,
`offset_phi` in degrees,
`ratio_lambda/2`, unitless.
